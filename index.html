About Botsogo Ba Rona

Our Mission

Botsogo Ba Rona is a resourceful and innovative non-profit organization with a mission to educate and empower South Africans to make responsible life choices. We facilitate the implementation of interventions that are evidence-informed and rights-based, with a strong focus on sexual and reproductive health and rights (SRHR).

Since 2001, we have been a women-led and managed organization, dedicated to strengthening health systems and building capacity within vulnerable communities. We operate in provinces across South Africa, from the Eastern Cape to the Western Cape, bringing sustainable social change to where it's needed most.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering communities and health staff.
  • Innovation: We find new ways to address health challenges.
  • Accountability: We operate with transparency for measurable results.

Our Programs and Expertise

Our work is focused on creating a society where every individual, especially adolescents and youth, has the knowledge and access to services to lead a healthy and productive life.

Adolescent & Youth Friendly Clinic Program

We design and implement youth-friendly services tailored to the unique needs of adolescents (10-14) and young adults (15-24).

HIV/AIDS and TB Care

We deliver comprehensive, compassionate care for adolescents and young people living with HIV and their families.

Peer Education Programme

Our peer education program empowers young people to become leaders in their communities.

Comprehensive Abortion Care

We build the capacity of both communities and health systems to enable free, early, and stigma-free access to safe abortion services.

Menstrual Health Management

We work to address the myths, taboos, and stigma surrounding menstruation.

General SRHR Services

We strengthen primary health care clinics and community health workers so that more people have access to the information and services they need.
